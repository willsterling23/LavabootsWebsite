About


Lavaboots Studios is a team of passionate game developers dedicated to creating immersive open-world experiences. Headquartered in Huntsville, Al, Lavaboots has been making games for over a decade and is responsible for three commercially successful titles: Salt 2, Salt, and Wild West and Wizards.
